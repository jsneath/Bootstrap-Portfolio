About me

Hello, I'm James from Kent, UK. My journey into the world of web development began with an aspiration to become a proficient front-end web developer. Currently, I'm immersed in a front-end bootcamp, honing my skills in the foundational pillars of web development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the dynamic framework of React. Away from the rigours of development, I find solace in the culinary world, experimenting with cooking and tasting interesting food wherever i can. I'm an avid football enthusiast, a voracious learner, and a fitness aficionado. My penchant for history often takes me down memory lanes, and my love for travel ensures I'm always on the lookout for a new horizon to explore. Join me on my journey as I code, cook, and explore the myriad facets of life.
